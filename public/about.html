About JRG News



JRG NEWS is a leading news website committed to delivering unbiased news coverage from around the world. Our team works tirelessly to bring you the latest developments in politics, business, technology, and other areas of interest. At JRG NEWS, we believe that access to accurate information is essential for making informed decisions and shaping our world. That's why we adhere to the highest standards of integrity, ensuring that our sources are objective, factual, and free from any political or commercial bias. We strive to provide our readers with a wide range of perspectives on important issues, offering in-depth analysis and expert opinions from a diverse range of voices. We believe that this approach not only fosters a better understanding of the world but also promotes healthy debate and civil discourse. Whether you're looking for breaking news, investigative reporting, or insightful commentary, JRG NEWS has got you covered.

JRG News aims to remove bias from your media and deliver information from only the most reliable sources on the web as well as create a pleasant and simple experience for all our users. We provide language translation, background color, font color, font resize and font changing capabilities when reading your article. We also have a newsletter which would give you the latest headlines of the week, based on the topic preference you choose. Our advanced search allows you to search for certain keywords, whether it be in the title or the summary or both, how recent you want news, and how you would want it to be sorted.

Methodology

We have done extensive research and refered to many different Source critics, such as AdFontes Media, Media Bias/Fact Checker, and AllSides, to anchor ourselves and determine which sources we could trust the most. All of these resources have been cross-referenced and hand-picked based on a criteria we set for ourselves. We have over 50 sources that are the least-biased and most factual.
