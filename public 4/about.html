JRG News aims to remove bias from your media and deliver information from only the most reliable sources on the web as well as create a pleasant and simple experience for all our users. We provide language translation, background color, font color, font resize and font changing capabilities when reading your article. We also have a newsletter which would give you the latest headlines of the week, based on the topic preference you choose. Our advanced search allows you to search for certain keywords, whether it be in the title or the summary or both, how recent you want news, and how you would want it to be sorted. We have done extensive research and have used many different Source critics, such as AdFontes Media, Media Bias/Fact Checker, and AllSides, to anchor ourselves and determine which sources we could trust the most. We have over 50 sources that are the least-biased and most factual. This project was created as our 2023 Capstone at Adelphi University. It is powered by NewsCatcherAPI and NewsParserAPI, and is hosted by Firebase.
